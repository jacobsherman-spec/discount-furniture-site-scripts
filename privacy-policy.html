2. Information This GPT May Process

When you use the Discount Furniture GPT, the GPT may process information that you provide in the chat or through connected actions. This may include:

  • Product names, SKUs, descriptions, categories, pricing, supplier information, and inventory-related details
  • Website content, HTML product descriptions, image URLs, and brand-template information
  • Business workflow information related to Discount Furniture operations
  • Customer information only if you choose to provide it, such as names, emails, phone numbers, delivery notes, or order-related details
  • Technical information needed to complete an action, such as API requests, product IDs, preview data, or update confirmations

3. How Information Is Used

Information provided to this GPT is used only to complete the task requested by the user. This may include:

  • Creating or editing product descriptions
  • Formatting website content for Lightspeed / Ecwid
  • Preparing inventory, pricing, or supplier-related workflows
  • Previewing or updating product information through approved connected actions
  • Generating business documents, marketing copy, or operational content
  • Helping troubleshoot website, GitHub, Cloudflare, or Lightspeed-related workflows

We do not sell personal information. We do not use information submitted to this GPT for unrelated marketing purposes.

4. Connected Services and Actions

This GPT may connect to third-party services or internal tools to complete requested tasks. These may include services such as OpenAI, GitHub, Cloudflare, Lightspeed, or other business systems used by Discount Furniture.

When a connected action is used, only the information reasonably necessary to complete that action should be sent to the connected service. For example, a product-description update may require sending a SKU, product ID, current description, replacement HTML, or confirmation data.

Information sent through third-party services may also be handled according to those services’ own privacy policies and terms.

5. Customer Information

Users should avoid submitting sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for the requested task. If customer information is provided, it should only be used for the specific business purpose requested, such as preparing an email, reviewing an order issue, creating delivery notes, or assisting with customer service.

This GPT should not be used to collect payment card numbers, Social Security numbers, bank account numbers, medical information, or other highly sensitive personal information.

6. Data Storage

Discount Furniture does not intentionally store chat conversations from this GPT outside of the systems needed to complete requested actions. However, certain connected services may temporarily process or retain technical logs, request history, or action results for security, troubleshooting, auditing, or system reliability.

If an action updates a business system, such as a product description, inventory record, website file, or audit log, that update may remain stored in the applicable business system.

7. Data Sharing

Information may be shared with connected services only when needed to complete the user’s requested task. This may include:

  • Lightspeed, if product, inventory, or pricing-related actions are used
  • Other tools specifically connected to the GPT for business operations

Discount Furniture does not sell information submitted through this GPT.

8. Security

Reasonable steps are taken to limit access to connected systems and reduce unnecessary data sharing. Connected actions should use appropriate authentication, permissions, and confirmation steps before making business changes.

No system can guarantee complete security. Users should avoid submitting confidential, unnecessary, or highly sensitive information through the GPT.
